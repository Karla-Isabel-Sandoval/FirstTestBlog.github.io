Future Projects-Interests-Aspirations

Future projects and interests include: Working on products related to self data quantification-such as fit bits. Like Justin said this morning-"back pocket technology", I too am interested in working on handless technology that doesn't steel the moment from people but allows one to stay present while making good use of the data we produce throughout the day to learn more about our behavior patterns-such as eating patterns,distractions,mood changes,sleeping patterns,stress, motivation,persistence and dependence(including drugs..yes including tobacco and joe). Geomapping and Georesearch-I would also love to work on maps. Maps are one of the apps that I use most on my phone and improving them would be short of awesome. Lastly I am also interested in learning more about productivity and time management products that are out there-prior to starting at TC my app idea was one based on tracking actual behavior and schedule behaviors versus those tracked on a planner to make an alarm/buzzer app that helps retrain behavior and patterns based on the sound/ and motion trigger.
